About

Where I've been
I was born in Lublin, south-east Poland and raised in a picaresque town of Chelm close to the Polish-Ukrainian border. Inquisitive, energetic and independent I've always been busy learning new things and putting them into practice in many different contexts. Passionate about the diversity of cultures around the world and the way people from these cultures communicate with one another, I started learning foreign languages (English and Russian to begin with) and ended up graduating with MA in Teaching English as a Foreign Language from the University of Humanities and Economics in Lodz and MRes in English Literature and Creative Writing from the University of Birmingham. In the meantime I managed to gain a lot of valuable experience working in many different jobs ranging from baby-sitting and decorating interiors to preparing students to sit their IELTS academic exams both in Poland and abroad.

What I am doing
Life keeps me very busy these days. My main focus at the moment is my ten-month-old son who each and every day inspires me to keep on moving forward. Apart from being a new mum I am also a very down-to-earth full time primary school teacher who, not only makes sure that all Year 3 children know their times tables and spellings by heart, but also manages EAL pupils across the whole school and acts as a MFL subject lead. In my spare time I sell vintage clothing and accessories online and being proper OCD constantly redesign and organize my house and garden.

What I hope to do

Being constantly hungry for knowledge and new challenges, I decided that it is the right time to reinvent my professional career. Thus I signed up to Coding Bootcamp run by the University of Birmingham and, as hopefully the website you are just looking at proves, rapidly learn coding. I hope that the skills gained at Bootcamp will let me find employment in IT sector and deepen my knowledge about software development.
